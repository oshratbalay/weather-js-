Multicolor gradient wallpapers pack for iPhone

Jim Gresham ∙ Updated April 27, 2021

Despite being completely minimalist, gradient wallpapers are some of the most popularly downloaded images on the iDownloadBlog Wallpapers of the Week gallery. This collection features extremely smooth gradient transitions in single colors. From hot pink to dark turquoise, there is a color for every setup.

Gradient wallpapers for iPhone

The following gradient wallpaper collection is a 19×9 ratio, perfect for a variety of mobile devices. At 3040×1440 it will still look fairly decent on a larger iPad screen with minimal tiling.

The set is an original collection by @MarcusBremen. This seven image collection combines a dark contrasting color way, which fades with an angle gradient to the top left. Care was taken in the color transition to prevent color banding, which is why we are offering them in the original .PNG file format, which should preserve the smooth gradient.

Marcus Bremen regularly updates the Twitter feed with regular downloads. From abstract geometry, to simple designs, there should always be something you can use for your setup.

My favorite gradient below is certainly the hot pink color way. The completely bold pink that fades into an extremely soft cotton candy pink offers the largest transition of color and shading in the collection.
